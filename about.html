Vagabond is a Japanese epic martial arts manga series written and illustrated by Takehiko Inoue. It is a fictionalized account of the life of Miyamoto Musashi, a legendary swordsman widely regarded as the greatest swordsman of Japan.

Inspired by Eiji Yoshikawa's novel Musashi, the manga depicts Musashi's journey from a wild and violent youth to a master swordsman seeking a path of enlightenment.

Vagabond is known for its stunning artwork, intricate fight sequences, and exploration of philosophical themes.
